A [blog](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blog) is a *web log*. Thus, I can write anything about what I read, think, and do here. However, I'm not a creative writer yet, so temporarily, most posts will be some notes I take while I'm reading books or articles.

There are many note-taking methods for reading: Mind map, Cornell, SQ3R, Zettelkasten, etc. I discovered that I'm very hard to insist on any one of these methods. So I don't apply any of them. Methodless is my method. I just jot down whatever sparks in my brain: quotes, reflections, summaries, connections, insights, etc. Imitating [*freewriting*](https://writingprocess.mit.edu/process/step-1-generate-ideas/instructions/freewriting/), I might call this method *free-note-taking*.
