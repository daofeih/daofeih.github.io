## Part I. Preliminaries

### Chapter 2. The Great Fundamental versus Technical Analysis Debate

> In fact, many of the world’s most successful traders use fundamental analysis to determine the direction to trade a market and technical analysis to time the entry and exit of such trades.

I think it's easier to determine the direction through technical analysis, so technical analysis is enough.

> To summarize, there is no “right” side to the great fundamental versus technical debate: the appropriate method depends on the individual.

I prefer technical analysis because fundamental analysis is too difficult to learn for me.
