*The Big Questions: A Short Introduction to Philosophy*, 9th Edition (2013) by Robert C. Solomon

## Preface

Philosophy gives a person the confidence to face the change of the world.

> Philosophy is simply *thinking hard* about life, about what we have learned, about our place in the world. Philosophy is, literally, the *love of wisdom*.

### The Subject of Philosophy

> But philosophy is not primarily the study of *other* people’s ideas. Philosophy is first of all the attempt to state clearly, and as convincingly and interestingly as possible, *your own* views. That is *doing* philosophy, not just reading about how someone else has done it.
